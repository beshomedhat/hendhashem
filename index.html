About

Turning a room into a stage for change

I am an arts facilitator and workshop designer based in Egypt, working at the intersection of participatory theatre, movement, and community development. My practice is grounded in the belief that creative tools can unlock emotional expression, foster connection, and drive real social change.

From refugee women in Aswan to at-risk youth in Cairo, from entrepreneurship camps to gender equality forums — I design experiences that meet communities where they are, and guide them toward where they want to be.

I hold a BA in Mass Communication (Radio & Television) from Cairo University, a Counselling School Diploma from Beit El-Moshawara, and I am currently completing the Community Activation Facilitators (CAF) programme.
